# idées ## grands principes de la gestion de projets * approche agile: - historique: dépasser l'effet "tunel" avec le développement: - cahier des charges - longue période de dev - livraison puis changements + difficile et nombreux car déconnection - décrire tache à l'aide de "user stories" (quand c'est possible: " It captures the 'who', 'what' and 'why' of a requirement in a simple, concise way, often limited in detail by what can be hand-written on a small paper notecard." - Planning is guessing => découper un gros projet en courtes itérations => planifier les taches d'une ité entre parties prenantes (product honor) => impliquer le "client" dans la planification des taches - stand up meeting quotidien pour avancement (max 10min) - meetings are toxic BUT face to face communication is the most efficient: => use the right tool for the right thing: - planning - writing docs w/ many people - broadcast info - ask questions (chat, forum like, etc) - email comes last => favor informal exchange where big ideas are discussed, weak ties are created, etc. - trust each other * Kanban board - formalisation des process de production dans les usines japonaises - visualiser le process de production (workflow) à l'aide de vignettes : TodoDoingDone - set limit to WIP * design thinking - result from a fusion of analytic (science) and synthesis approach (deisgners/architects) - scientists : - break out complex problems into several less complex pb - font des analyses poussées - problem-focused - designers : - rassembles d'abord les éléments de connaissances sur un pb - think of solution to try out - solution-focused REF :— Bryan Lawson, How Designers Think[11] - Design thinking process http://dschool.stanford.edu/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/steps-730x345.png -- design for human
